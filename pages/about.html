About Joe Camuglia:

A native of Marlboro, New York, a small farming community 60 miles north of the city, Joseph has been writing and singing songs since he was a teenager.

A troubadour who has brought his guitar strumming, harmonica playing sound to people from all walks of life.

From front porches and living rooms to small bars, country churches, subways, street corners, restaurants, café’s, schools, and festivals... Joseph has sung his way around this country, delighting folks of all ages.

He has graced the stage of Pete Seeger’s Strawberry Festival as well as the 25th year reunion of The Woodstock Festival in Bethel, NY.

A prolific songwriter who has written well over 10 thousand songs, His songs cover a wide array of topics, played in a mostly folk and Americana style.

Joseph has several studio albums and many home recordings that are available. Additionally, he has thousands of songs available for artists who may want to record them for their own project!

Presently based in Lexington, Kentucky where he shares his life with his jewelry making wife, Amy who, along with his children, sometimes accompanies him.
